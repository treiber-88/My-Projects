The History of the Yuri

By James E. Drzycimski

Yuri began his career as a psychic manipulator and general threat to humanity during the Second World War, known as Red Alert 1. He was of Romanian descent and inherited a familial castle in Transylvanial, where he would carry out his personal war with the Allies, even when the Soviets and the Allies were at peace.

Yuri commands his forces from his ancestral home in... Transylvania! He is like monster from movies, yes!?

- Alexander Romanov

After the Soviets were defeated by the Allies in the second World War, Yuri joined the Allied puppet Alexander Romanov, known as Premier Romanov. From then on it was mostly like him, not the premier, who was the real leader of the Soviets. He was probably the cause of the Third World War, or Red Alert 2.

This is my Psychic beacon. With it, we can send any message we want. We will construct this device everywhere. In the end, the Americans will be ours. Mind, body, and if you'd like, soul.

-Yuri

There are many alternate fates of Yuri. According to the Allied Campaign he was cornered into his castle where he half succesfully tried to time travel back in time. He did, but to the wrong era. Some say that he was sent to the Crestacious and was eaten by a dinosaur, but as people know, the earth is only six thousand years old. He was sent back to the Medievel Era, and is probably still there, happily toasting the health of his friend Edward I.
